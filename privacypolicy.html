Last updated: February 4, 2026

By using our repository or related pages, you agree to the collection and use of information described in this policy.

We use collected information for purposes including:

We do not directly set cookies on static repository pages. The platform hosting these pages (for example, GitHub) or any analytics services you encounter via linked pages may use cookies or similar tracking technologies. Please review the privacy and cookie policies of those third-party providers for details.

We rely on third-party services for hosting, continuous integration, package distribution, analytics, and other functions. We will not sell your personal information. We may disclose information when required by law, to respond to legal requests or to protect our rights, safety, or property. Aggregated or anonymized data that does not identify individuals may be shared publicly to help improve the project.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information under our control. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of any information you provide or that third parties collect.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights concerning your personal data, such as the right to access, correct, delete, or export your information. To exercise these rights, please open an issue on the repository or contact the repository owner via the project's GitHub page.

The GooseSupreme project and its repository pages are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under this age. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us so we can take appropriate steps.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above will indicate when changes were made. Continued use of the project pages after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request action regarding your data, please open an issue on the GooseSupreme repository or visit the project page on GitHub to contact the repository owner.
